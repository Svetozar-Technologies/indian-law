## Section 42 - Penalty for breach of rules.

Whoever breaks any rule made or continued under this Act, not
being a rule made by the ^1[High Court], shall, on conviction before a Magistrate; be punishable with fine
which may extend to fifty rupees, or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to six months, or
with both.
